Chicken Picatta

Background

Chicken Picatta is an Italian American dish consisting of pan fried

chicken breasts served in a lemon and caper sauce, typically over a bed of rice.

The Recipe

Ingredients

  • Butter 3 Tbsp
  • Boneless Skinless Chicken Breast 1/4 lb
  • Flour 1 Heaping Tablespoon
  • Olive Oil 1 Tbsp
  • Shallot 1/2 finely chopped
  • Galic 3 Cloves Minced
  • Chicken/Vegetable Stock 3/4 Cup
  • Lemon Zest 1 Large Pinch
  • Juice of 1/2 Lemon
  • Capers 1 Tbsp

Steps

  1. Pound or split Chicken Breast and add to a bag with Flour and season with salt and pepper. Shaking to coat.
  2. Melt 1 Tbsp Butter and Oil.
  3. Add Chicken Breast and fry until golden brown.
  4. Remove Chicken Breast and set aside.
  5. Add a touch more Oil and saute Shallots.
  6. Add Garlicand saute until fragrant.
  7. Add Stock and bring to a simmer. Cook until reduced by half.
  8. Add Lemon Zest, Lemon Juice, Capers, and remaining 2 Tbsp of Butter.
  9. Return Chicken Breast to pan and cook until heated.
  10. Serve over rice.
