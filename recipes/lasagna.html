Lasagna

Background

Lasagna was introduced in Naples at the height of the black plague.

Originally reserved for special events this pasta cake makes a great mid week dinner.

The Recipe

Ingredients

  • Uncooked Lasagna Noodles 12
  • Shredded Mozzarella 4 Cups
  • Shredded Parmesan 1/2 Cup

Tomato Sauce

  • Lean Ground Beef 1/2 Lb
  • Italian Sausage 1/2 Lb
  • Onion 1
  • Minced Garic 3 Cloves
  • Pasta Sauce 36 Ounces
  • Tomato Paste 2 Tbsp
  • Italian Seasoning 1 Tsp

Cheese Mixture

  • Ricotta 2 Cups
  • Beaten Egg 1
  • Chopped Parsley 1/4 Cup

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven to 350 F. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Add olive oil to water along with Lasagna Noodles and cook al dente
  3. In a large skillet brown Beef, Sausage, Onion,and Garlic over medium high heat. Drain fat.
  4. Stir in Pasta Sauce, Tomato Paste, Italian Seasoning,salt and pepper.
  5. Simmer uncovered until thickened.
  6. In a bowl combine 1 1/2 Cups of Mozzarella 1/4 Cup Parmesan, Ricotta, Parsley, Egg, and salt.
  7. In a casserole dish, put down a layer of meat sauce followed by a layer of lasagna noodles then a layer of cheese mixture.
  8. Repeat until lasagna is built.
  9. Cover with foil and bake for 45 minutes.
  10. Remove foil and top with remaining 2 1/2 Cups Mozzarella Cheese and 1/4 Cup Parmesan
  11. Bake an additional 15 minutes.
