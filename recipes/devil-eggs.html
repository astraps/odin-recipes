Deviled Eggs

Background

Deviled Eggs can be traced back to the Roman empire and are

no less delicious today. The perfect addition to any barbeque.

The Recipe

Ingredients

  • Eggs Half Dozen
  • Mustard 1 Tsp
  • Tobasco 1-2 Dashes
  • Mayonnaise 3 Tbsp
  • Paprika

Steps

  1. Hard boil Eggs. Cover with cold water and bring to a boil, turn off heat, cover and let sit for 10-15 minutes. Drain, rinse in cold water, and peel. Let cool in refrigerator.
  2. Half Eggs lengthwise, separate yolks to a bowl.
  3. Add Mustard, Tobasco, salt and pepper. Stir in Mayonnaise
  4. Fill plastic bag with yolk mixture, cut off corner and pipe into halved eggs.
  5. Dust with Paprika.
